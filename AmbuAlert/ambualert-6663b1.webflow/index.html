FAQs

Find answers to commonly asked questions about AmbuAlert and its deployment

How does AmbuAlert work?

AmbuAlert integrates real-time alerts into map software, providing drivers and pedestrians with proactive notifications about approaching ambulances in urban traffic. This enables them to make way and ensure faster emergency response times.

Is AmbuAlert available worldwide?

Currently, AmbuAlert is available in select cities. However, our goal is to expand its deployment to more locations in the future, ensuring the safety and efficiency of emergency services globally.

How can I access AmbuAlert?

AmbuAlert can be accessed through compatible map software applications. Simply download the app, enable the AmbuAlert feature, and start receiving real-time alerts about approaching ambulances.

Is AmbuAlert free to use?

Yes, AmbuAlert is currently available for free. However, there may be premium features or subscription plans in the future to enhance the user experience and support the continuous development of the service.

How can I provide feedback?

We value your feedback! You can provide feedback through the AmbuAlert app or contact our support team directly. We appreciate your input in helping us improve the service.
